OutfitKart Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

1. Information Hamare Paas

Jab aap OutfitKart use karte hain, hum yeh information collect karte hain: Mobile Number (required for login), Full Name, Email Address (optional), Delivery Address, Order History, Wallet Transactions, Referral Data, aur Push Notification Subscription.

2. Information Ka Use

Aapki information ka use: Orders process karne ke liye, Delivery coordinate karne ke liye, Customer support dene ke liye, Wallet aur referral earnings calculate karne ke liye, Push notifications bhejne ke liye (sirf aapki permission se), aur App improve karne ke liye kiya jaata hai.

3. Data Security

Aapka data Supabase (enterprise-grade security) par store hota hai. Passwords hashed form mein store hote hain. Hum aapka data kabhi bhi third parties ko sell ya share nahi karte — sirf delivery partners ko order fulfillment ke liye zaruri information di jaati hai.

4. Payment Security

Payments Razorpay ke through process hote hain jo RBI-approved payment gateway hai. Hum koi bhi card/UPI credentials store nahi karte. Wallet transactions encrypted aur secure hain.

5. Push Notifications

Push notifications sirf aapki explicit permission se bheje jaate hain. Aap kabhi bhi Profile → Notifications OFF karke unsubscribe kar sakte hain. Hum spam nahi karte — sirf important updates (order status, special offers) bhejte hain.

6. Cookies & Local Storage

Hum Cart, Session, aur Recently Viewed products localStorage mein store karte hain for better user experience. Yeh data sirf aapke device par hota hai.

7. Aapke Rights

Aapko yeh rights hain: Account delete karne ka right, Data correction ka right, Data export ka right. In rights ke liye [email protected] par contact karein.

8. Contact

Privacy related queries ke liye: [email protected] ya WhatsApp: +91 9182296773

Return & Refund Policy

Unused, unwashed items 7 days ke andar return kar sakte hain. Damaged ya defective items pe full refund milega. COD orders mein ₹9 handling fee non-refundable hai. Exchange same ya higher value products ke liye available hai.

Referral Terms

Referral commission 5% of order total hai. Commission 30 days baad confirm hota hai (agar order cancel na ho). Fake/self-referrals pe account ban ho sakta hai.

Wallet Terms

Minimum withdrawal ₹120 hai. Withdrawal 24-48 hours mein process hoti hai. Wallet balance non-transferable hai.
